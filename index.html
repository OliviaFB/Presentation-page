About me

Hi! From a very young age, I have been wanting to discover the world, which led me to study international business, learn different languages and live in several countries, among which Russia and the USA. In my last year of studies I ended up in Amsterdam for my master's internship. Nine years later, I am still in the Netherlands! My professional background includes sales, marketing, recruitment and since 2021: web development!
I first encountered the basics of HTML and styling in my teenage years, when I was holding a blog. Although I didn't pursue this field back then, today I am convinced this is the right path for me. 2020 marks the year I took a leap. I applied for the Le Wagon bootcamp and spent nine unforgettable weeks.
During this intensive training, I discovered that my interest in frontend development was dominant. Playing with design features and spending hours digging into Javascript lines is what I like. With that in mind, I have decided to look for a job in frontend development.
