About me

Hi! From a very young age, I have been wanting to discover the world, which led me to study international business. I had the chance to fullfil my dream of doing exchange semesters in Russia and the USA. In my last year of studies I ended up in Amsterdam for my master's internship. Eight years later, I am still in the Netherlands! My professional background includes sales, marketing recruitment and since 2021: web development!
I first encountered the basics of HTML and styling in my teenage years, when I was holding a blog. Although I didn't pursue in this field then, today I am convinced this is the right path for me. 2020 marks the year I took a leap and decided to make the switch! Thankfully, studying at Le Wagon played a huge role to make it possible.
Following the intensive nine weeks of the Le Wagon bootcamp, my love for front-end and my desire to specialize took over. I am eager to join a company whose mission is to make an impact in their field. With that in mind, I have decided to look for a job in front-end development.
